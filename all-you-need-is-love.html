All You Need Is Love

2024 · Brand Design

This retro-inspired theme will create a world where all you need is love. After all, love is the foundation of the hope and educational resources that the Parent Child Center helps provide to make families stronger. With a nod to the peace and love era, this colorful concept will be a wonderful experience for our donors to hear stories of hope.

I managed the event from concept to completion in collaboration with my creative director. I developed initial ideas to the client, and once a direction was chosen, I designed all creative assets, including save the dates, invitations, social media posts, and large-format event graphics like step and repeats, bar fronts, and a custom Volkswagen bus cutout.
